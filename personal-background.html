Good afternoon! I'm Edmon Gutlay — 18 years old, born June 20, 2007, and raised in Project 4, Barangay Marilag, Quezon City. I'm the youngest of seven siblings.

Life hasn't always been easy, especially after losing my mother at 17. But she remains my biggest inspiration. I carry her with me every day — on and off the court.

I keep things simple: basketball, motorcycles, fruits. No sweets, no drama. I value discipline and a balanced life. I also enjoy editing basketball highlight videos on the side.
